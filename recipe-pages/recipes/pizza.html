Pizza Recipe

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy, characterized by its round, flat base made of dough topped with a variety of ingredients. The classic version features a thin or thick crust, smeared with a rich tomato sauce, and generously topped with mozzarella cheese. It can be customized with an array of toppings, including pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, and various meats or vegetables. Baked at high temperatures, pizza achieves a delightful combination of a crispy crust and melted cheese. Whether enjoyed at a pizzeria, made at home, or served at gatherings, pizza is a versatile and comforting favorite loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups (300g) all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) warm water (about 110°F or 43°C)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • For Toppings

  • Pepperoni slices
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Olives (black or green)
  • Cooked sausage, crumbled
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Red pepper flakes (for a spicy kick)

    • Steps to cook:

    Prepare the dough

    • You can use store-bought dough or make your own by mixing flour, yeast, water, and salt.
    • Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours in a warm place if homemade.

    Preheat the Oven

    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating to get a crispy crust.

    Roll out the Dough

    • Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired pizza shape (round or rectangular)
    • Make sure the dough is evenly thin for even cooking.

    Add Sauce

    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce (or any sauce you prefer) over the rolled-out dough.

    Add toppings

    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella or your favorite cheese over the sauce.
    • Add toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, or vegetables—whatever you like!

    Bake the Pizza

    • Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    • Bake for about 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

    Serve and Enjoy

    • Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and slice it up.
