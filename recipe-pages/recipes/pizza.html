Pizza Recipe

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy, characterized by its round, flat base made of dough topped with a variety of ingredients. The classic version features a thin or thick crust, smeared with a rich tomato sauce, and generously topped with mozzarella cheese. It can be customized with an array of toppings, including pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, and various meats or vegetables. Baked at high temperatures, pizza achieves a delightful combination of a crispy crust and melted cheese. Whether enjoyed at a pizzeria, made at home, or served at gatherings, pizza is a versatile and comforting favorite loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups (300g) all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) warm water (about 110°F or 43°C)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • For Toppings

  • Pepperoni slices
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Olives (black or green)
  • Cooked sausage, crumbled
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Red pepper flakes (for a spicy kick)

    • instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Dough

    1. Activate Yeast
      • In a small bowl, combine the warm water, sugar and yeast. Stir gently and let it slit for about 5-10 minutes until becomes frothy
    2. Mix the Dough
      • In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir with a wooden spoon until a dough starts to form.
    3. Knead the Dough
        Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    4. Let It Rise:
      • Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.

    Step 2:Prepare the Tomato Sauce

    1. Make the Sauce:
      • In a saucepan, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
      • Add the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for about 15 minutes to develop the flavors.

      Step 3: Assemble the Pizza

    1. Preheat the Oven:
      • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
    2. Shape the Dough
      • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it back to a floured surface. Divide it into two portions if you want two pizzas.
      • Roll out the dough with a rolling pin or stretch it by hand to your desired thickness (about 1/4 inch thick for a classic crust).
    3. Add the Sauce
      • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spread a thin layer of the prepared tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    4. Add Cheese and Toppings
        5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your choice of toppings, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or any other favorite ingredients.

    Step 4: Bake the Pizza

      1. Bake:
      2. Place the pizza in the preheated oven (on the pizza stone or baking sheet) and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

    Step 5:Serve

  • Cool and Slice:
      • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes. Then, slice it into wedges or squares.
  • Enjoy
    • Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil or a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
